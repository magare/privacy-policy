# Privacy Policy for DarkTube AI

**Last Updated: November 30, 2024**

## Overview

DarkTube AI ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the Extension handles your data.

## Data Collection

**DarkTube AI does not collect any personal data.**

The Extension operates entirely offline and does not:

- Collect personal information
- Track browsing history
- Record video viewing habits
- Send any data to external servers
- Use analytics or telemetry services
- Require user accounts or registration

## Data Storage

The Extension stores only one piece of data locally on your device:

| Data                                  | Purpose                      | Storage Location                   |
| ------------------------------------- | ---------------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Extension enabled/disabled preference | Remember your toggle setting | Chrome Local Storage (device only) |

This preference:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not synced to any cloud service
- Is not shared with any third party
- Can be cleared by uninstalling the extension

## Data Processing

### No External Communications

The Extension makes zero network requests. All functionality runs locally using:

- Local JavaScript processing
- Chrome's built-in storage API

## Third-Party Services

**None.** DarkTube AI does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services.

## Permissions Used

| Permission                      | Why It's Needed                                          |
| ------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------- |
| `activeTab`                     | To detect when you're on YouTube                         |
| `scripting`                     | To inject the dark mode functionality into YouTube pages |
| `storage`                       | To save your on/off preference locally                   |
| `host_permissions: youtube.com` | To run only on YouTube websites                          |

## Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

## Summary

- No data collection
- No tracking
- No external servers
- No accounts required
- 100% local processing
- Only stores one on/off preference on your device

**Your privacy is guaranteed because we simply don't collect any data.**
