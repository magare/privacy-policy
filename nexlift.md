# Privacy Policy for NexLift

**Last Updated: January 17, 2026**

## Introduction

NexLift ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how NexLift handles your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data Stored Locally

NexLift is designed with a **privacy-first, offline-first architecture**. All your data is stored **locally on your device** and is **never transmitted to external servers**.

The following data is stored locally on your device:

- **Workout Data**: Exercise logs, sets, reps, weights, and workout history
- **Progress Photos**: Optional body progress pictures (stored only on your device)
- **User Preferences**: App settings, unit preferences, timer configurations
- **Achievement Data**: XP points, workout streaks, and personal records

### Data We Do NOT Collect

NexLift does **NOT**:
- Collect any personal information
- Track your location
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Display advertisements
- Share data with third parties
- Transmit any data over the internet
- Require account registration

## Camera and Photo Access

NexLift requests camera and photo library access **only** for the optional body metrics progress photo feature. These photos:
- Are stored locally on your device only
- Are never uploaded or transmitted anywhere
- Can be deleted at any time from within the app or your device gallery

## Data Storage and Security

All data is stored using encrypted local storage on your device. Your data:
- Remains on your device at all times
- Is protected by your device's security measures
- Can be exported by you at any time (JSON or CSV format)
- Will be permanently deleted if you uninstall the app

## Export and Backup

You can export your workout data at any time through the Settings screen:
- **JSON Format**: Full backup of all workout data
- **CSV Format**: Spreadsheet-compatible format for external analysis

These exports are created locally and shared using your device's standard sharing functionality.

## Children's Privacy

NexLift does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is designed for fitness enthusiasts of all ages but is intended primarily for teens and adults.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control:
- **Access**: View all your data within the app
- **Export**: Export your data at any time
- **Delete**: Uninstall the app to remove all data, or clear app data in device settings
